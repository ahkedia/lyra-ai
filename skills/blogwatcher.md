# Blogwatcher - RSS Feed Integration

Monitor your RSS feeds (News Inbox, competitors, content sources) automatically.

## Configured Feeds

### News & Trends
- TechCrunch RSS
- Hacker News RSS
- Product Hunt RSS

### Competitors
- Monitor competitor blogs
- Industry news feeds

### Content Inspiration
- LinkedIn articles
- Medium trending
- Dev.to hot posts

## Usage

"Lyra, fetch the latest news" → Triggers RSS scan, summarizes top stories
"Add [URL] to my feeds" → Adds new RSS feed to blogwatcher
"What's trending today?" → Shows top 5 trending articles
