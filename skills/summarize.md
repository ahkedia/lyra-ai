# Summarize - Content Synthesis

Quickly distill important information from articles, emails, and documents.

## Usage

"Summarize this article in 3 bullets" → Creates concise summary
"TL;DR of [content]" → Quick summary
"What's the key insight from [source]?" → Extract main takeaway

## Configured For

- News articles (News Inbox)
- Competitor reports (Competitor Tracker)
- Email threads
- Long Notion documents
- Content drafts (editorial review)
